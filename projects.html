Intelligent Assessment and Emergency Repair of Transmission Towers Under Typhoon Disasters

AI-driven damage assessment using drone imagery and deep learning algorithms for rapid post-typhoon transmission tower damage identification. Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL) optimizes emergency resource dispatch, significantly reducing repair time and improving grid resilience.

NATIONAL GRID
Tower-Line System Failure Mechanism and Intelligent Resilience Enhancement
¥1,806,000 CNY (~$250,000 USD) | State Grid Zhejiang Electric Power
High-precision digital twin modeling of tower-line systems, analyzing coupled vibration effects under strong winds. Revealing collapse patterns and weak links of transmission towers under extreme wind loads. Developing resilience enhancement technologies based on new materials and structural optimization.

Role: Principal Investigator 2025-2026
