PINN+MARL Intelligent Wind Disaster Assessment

电网台风PINN+MARL智能评估系统
Client: State Grid Zhejiang Wenzhou Budget: ¥2,175,000 (~£240K) Period: 2025-2026 Role: Principal Investigator

项目概述 / Project Overview

本项目针对电网输电塔-线路系统在台风灾害下的安全评估需求，开发了基于物理信息神经网络（PINN）和多智能体强化学习（MARL）的智能评估系统。项目整合气象数据、结构健康监测数据和地理信息系统，实现台风路径预测、结构响应分析和应急资源调度的全流程智能化。

This project addresses the critical need for safety assessment of power transmission tower-line systems under typhoon conditions. We developed an intelligent assessment system combining Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINN) and Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL). The system integrates meteorological data, structural health monitoring, and geographic information systems to achieve full-process intelligence for typhoon path prediction, structural response analysis, and emergency resource dispatch.

商业价值 / Business Value

  • 48-hour advance warning of typhoon damage to power grid infrastructure, enabling proactive emergency preparation
  • Reduces emergency response costs by 80% through predictive risk assessment and optimized resource allocation
  • Prevents £10M+ annual losses from power outages by prioritizing high-risk infrastructure for reinforcement

技术方案 / Technical Approach

  • Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINN): Integrates governing physical equations (Navier-Stokes for wind field, structural dynamics for tower response) into neural network training, ensuring physically consistent predictions even with limited sensor data
  • Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL): Coordinates multiple emergency response teams (repair crews, equipment dispatch, logistics) through decentralized decision-making, optimizing response time under dynamic disaster conditions
  • Digital Twin Integration: Real-time synchronization between physical transmission towers and virtual models enables continuous model updating and prediction refinement as typhoon evolves
