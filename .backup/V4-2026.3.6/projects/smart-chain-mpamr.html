Smart Chain Cloud Construction / MPAMR Robot Platform

Type Industry Funded
Duration 2023-2025
Role Principal Investigator
¥1.37M Funding

Project Overview

This innovative project develops the Smart Chain Cloud Construction platform and Multi-Purpose Autonomous Mobile Robot (MPAMR) for intelligent construction applications. The system combines cloud-based construction management with autonomous robotics to revolutionize building processes through automation and digital integration.

Business Value

Construction automation and robotics integration deliver significant productivity enhancement, improved workplace safety, and reduced labor costs while enabling 24/7 operations and precision construction.

Key Technologies

Cloud Construction Platform

Centralized cloud-based system for construction planning, scheduling, and real-time monitoring across multiple job sites.

Autonomous Mobile Robotics

Multi-purpose robots with autonomous navigation, obstacle avoidance, and collaborative operation capabilities.

Digital Twin Integration

Real-time synchronization between physical robots and virtual models for remote monitoring and control.

Collaborative Robotics

Dual-arm robotic systems capable of complex construction tasks with human-robot collaboration.

System Capabilities

• Autonomous Navigation: SLAM-based navigation in dynamic construction environments

• Multi-Robot Coordination: Collaborative task execution among multiple robots

• Cloud-Based Control: Remote operation and monitoring via cloud platform

• Real-time Synchronization: Digital twin updates with <100ms latency

• Modular Design: Interchangeable end-effectors for diverse construction tasks

• Safety Systems: Comprehensive collision avoidance and emergency stop mechanisms

Expected Outcomes

• Productivity Gain: 50% increase in construction efficiency through automation

• Safety Improvement: 70% reduction in hazardous human exposure

• Quality Consistency: 95%+ precision in repetitive construction tasks

• Operational Flexibility: 24/7 operation capability with remote management
