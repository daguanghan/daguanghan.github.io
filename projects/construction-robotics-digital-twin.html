Construction Robotics Digital Twin Testbed

Type Industry Funded
Duration 2023-2025
Role Principal Investigator
Research Demonstrator

Project Overview

This research demonstrator develops a digital-twin-enabled construction robotics testbed for intelligent construction applications. The work combines collaborative robotics, control systems, and virtual-physical synchronisation to study construction automation in a controlled laboratory setting.

Business Value

The demonstrator supports teaching, prototyping, and research on construction automation, human-robot collaboration, quality control, and safer work methods.

Key Technologies

Construction Data Platform

Data environment for construction planning, scheduling, sensing, and monitoring in laboratory demonstrations.

Robotics Control

Robotics control workflows for navigation, task sequencing, and supervised operation in controlled environments.

Digital Twin Integration

Synchronisation between physical equipment and virtual models for monitoring, analysis, and teaching demonstrations.

Collaborative Robotics

Human-robot collaboration concepts for construction tasks, safety-aware planning, and repeatable quality workflows.

System Capabilities

• Construction automation: task sequencing and supervised robotic operation

• Digital twin synchronisation: virtual-physical state tracking for teaching and research

• Human-robot collaboration: safety-aware workflows and repeatable quality procedures

• Data-driven control: integration of sensor data, model state, and operational rules

Expected Outcomes

• A reusable teaching and research demonstrator for construction robotics and digital twins

• Prototype workflows for linking physical equipment, BIM data, and operational decision support

• Evidence for future collaboration in digital engineering, robotics, and smart construction education
