Thanks for stopping by my little corner of the internet!

I'm a Professor and Researcher passionate about Infrastructure Resilience, Digital Twins, and AI-Driven Disaster Mitigation. I bridge civil engineering and artificial intelligence to build resilient infrastructure for the future.

I lead research on digital twin modeling for critical infrastructure, intelligent structural health monitoring, and smart construction technologies. I founded the UltraEvolve platform and have published extensively in top-tier journals.

With a Ph.D. from Universität der Bundeswehr München and over 70 peer-reviewed publications (h-index: 13, 838+ citations), I've developed expertise in multi-physics simulation, physics-informed neural networks, and human-robot collaboration in construction.

Beyond academia, I'm an author of "Humanity and the Cosmos: Awakening the Universe Within" and currently writing on philosophical engineering and cosmic civil engineering.
