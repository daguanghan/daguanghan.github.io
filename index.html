I lead research on digital twin modeling for critical infrastructure, intelligent structural health monitoring, and smart construction technologies. I founded the UltraEvolve platform and have published extensively in top-tier journals including Energy and Buildings, Computer-Aided Civil and Infrastructure Engineering, and Engineering Structures.

Digital Twins Structural Health Monitoring AI & Machine Learning Smart Construction Infrastructure Resilience

With a Ph.D. from Universität der Bundeswehr München and over 70 peer-reviewed publications, I've developed expertise in multi-physics simulation, physics-informed neural networks, and human-robot collaboration in construction. My research has been applied to 50+ infrastructure projects including bridges, transmission towers, and airports.

Beyond academia, I'm an author of "Humanity and the Cosmos: Awakening the Universe Within" and currently writing on philosophical engineering and cosmic civil engineering.
