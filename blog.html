Infrastructure resilience

Why AI-enabled infrastructure resilience matters for UK civil engineering

Infrastructure owners need defensible evidence for maintenance and investment decisions. AI is useful when it is connected to engineering constraints, sensing evidence and accountable judgement.

Digital twins

Digital twins as research infrastructure, not only software platforms

The research value of a digital twin is not the model itself; it is the repeatable evidence workflow that links measurement, simulation, decision making and verification.

Teaching

Research-led teaching in digital engineering and infrastructure data

Students learn digital engineering most effectively when they work from messy measurement data to clear engineering decisions, rather than only learning software operation.
